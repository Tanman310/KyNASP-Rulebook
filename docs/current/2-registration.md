# Article 2 KyNASP State Bullseye Tournament Registration Protocol

## 2.1.
In addition to #1.1.-1.12. above.

## 2.2. KyNASP State Tournament (insert dates here)

### 2.2.1.
Qualification tiers will be established for the event.

### 2.2.2.
Team and Individual qualifications for the state bullseye tournament are only possible through participation in KyNASP-approved state qualifying tournaments.

### 2.2.3.
Team and Individual qualifications for the state bullseye tournament are determined by participation in at least one KyNASP state qualifying tournament.

### 2.2.4.
A qualified team (school/division) may register to participate in the state bullseye tournament. (A school team’s best state qualifying tournament score posted by ***insert time and date*** will be used to determine eligibility for participation and qualification/registration tier. High school team scores of ***insert score*** or more, middle school team scores of insert score or more, and elementary team scores of ***insert score*** or more will have the opportunity to register for the ***insert year*** KyNASP State Bullseye Tournament.)

### 2.2.5.
A qualified individual may register to participate in the KyNASP State Bullseye tournament. (A school individual’s best state qualifying tournament score posted by ***insert time and date*** will be used to determine eligibility for participation and qualification/registration tier if they are not with an advancing school team. Individuals in high school with a state qualifying tournament score of ***insert score*** or higher, middle school with a state qualifying tournament score of ***insert score*** or higher, and elementary school with a state qualifying tournament score of ***insert score*** or higher will have the opportunity to register for the ***insert year*** KyNASP State Bullseye Tournament).

### 2.2.6.
Qualified individuals may participate as an individual if their team does not qualify for advancement to state.

### 2.2.7.
Participation is not guaranteed in any KyNASP state tournament event. Flight positions are limited.

## 2.3.
Substitutions are permitted for these events. Substitutes may be students who did not participate as part of their state qualifying tournament team, but they must meet the criteria defined in section 1.

### 2.3.1.
Individuals must shoot in their actual division for the state tournament.

### 2.3.2.
If a school sent multiple teams to state qualifying tournaments, they may recombine archers from these teams for state. **Not applicable to entire school districts/systems.**

## 2.4.
A school may register as many teams as it has qualified at its state tournament, if the state in question allows multiple teams from the same school to participate.

#### 2.4.0.1. Kentucky Addendum
Second teams will be allowed to register for the ***insert year*** KyNASP State Bullseye Tournament (both teams must have state-qualifying scores of ***insert score*** high, ***insert score*** middle, and ***insert score*** elementary at the same state-qualifying tournament). Keep in mind that second teams will be placed in one of the last tiers for flight selection, making it difficult to receive the same flight time or flight day as first teams. Participation is not guaranteed at the state tournament. Flight positions are limited. 

## 2.5.
NASP teams and individual archers may only participate in a NASP state tournament in the state where their school is located.
